Professional Background

Simon Bacon has dedicated his entire professional career to child support law and advisory services — a field he entered after graduating from the University of Melbourne Law School and discovering that few practitioners understood its complexities.

After serving as Principal Lawyer at Legal Aid Queensland's child support division, Simon established a specialist practice at Normanby Chambers in Melbourne, building a national client base spanning every Australian state and territory. His focus has always been exclusively on child support — assessment disputes, agreements, enforcement, overseas matters, and administrative review.

In 2025, Simon transitioned from traditional legal practice to an advisory model through Manby & Scott Child Support Advisory Services. This approach delivers the same depth of specialist guidance through administrative channels — binding agreements, internal reviews, the CDDA scheme, and tribunal matters — at typically one-third the cost of a law firm.
