Simon Bacon

Independent Child Support Advisor

Melbourne, Victoria, Australia

Background

Simon Bacon advises on child support matters across Australia, drawing on experience that spans the full breadth of the system — from frontline Legal Aid casework in Queensland to running a national specialist practice from Melbourne's Normanby Chambers.

He now operates through an advisory model, helping parents and professionals navigate assessments, agreements and enforcement through administrative channels rather than litigation.
